  • Retention Challenges: A lack of supportive ecosystems for family life in high-stress engineering roles often forces women out of the industry.

1. Continuous Learning: Knowledge must grow beyond university levels to keep pace with rapid technological evolution.

2. Redefining Failure: View every failure as a "data point" rather than a dead end.

3. Resilience: Developing a "thick skin" to handle being underestimated while focusing strictly on technical delivery.

  • Decision-Making Strategy: She advises "sleeping on" difficult decisions rather than responding immediately, allowing logic to balance emotion.
