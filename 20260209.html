  • Historical Context: Clarida references the 1951 Accord, which established Fed independence by ending the practice of capping Treasury yields to support WWII debt.

  • Confirmation Outlook: Warsh is expected to be confirmed due to high qualifications, though the hearing process may be delayed.

      • "The Doom Loop": Clarida acknowledges recent theories (such as those by Eswar Prasad) suggesting the global economy may be entering a "non-linear" period of instability that standard linear macro models cannot capture.
