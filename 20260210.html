The Evolution from Prompting to "Environments"

      • The Rubric: A reward function that grades the quality of outputs and measures progress toward a goal.
  • The "30-Year Expert" Analogy: While prompting provides a "smart person" to help, training in an environment allows a model to gain the equivalent of "30 years of experience" by deeply interacting with a specific problem over time.
